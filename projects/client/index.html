Welcome to My Music Portfolio

What got me into music? I’ve been singing all my life, I was a very talkative kid so learning how to sing made my voice more pleasing to hear. Why did I start taking it serious? One of my friends actually showed me a song of his and it made me realize I could do the same thing if I put my mind to it, and it started from there. What is my goal for music? I don’t really have a goal for music, I just want to make art that can inspire others and that people can relate to.
